Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about traveling in Malaysia and Southeast Asia

Do you offer domestic Malaysian tours?

Yes! We specialize in Malaysian domestic tours covering all states from Perlis to Johor, including Sabah and Sarawak. We offer packages for Langkawi, Penang, Cameron Highlands, and many more destinations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Malaysian Ringgit payments via bank transfer, credit cards (Visa/Mastercard), online banking, and popular e-wallets like GrabPay and Touch 'n Go eWallet.

Do you provide visa assistance for international travel?

Absolutely! Our team assists Malaysian travelers with visa applications for popular destinations like Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, and other countries in our packages.

Are your tour guides Malaysian?

Yes, we work with licensed Malaysian tour guides who are knowledgeable about local culture, history, and speak multiple languages including Bahasa Malaysia, English, Mandarin, and Tamil.
